The Creator!

Hi! I'm Brooke Springer, I prefer going by my nickname Apricity. I really like drawing and coding, so I decided to combine the two in this program.

Where did the Swooshy come from?

The Swooshy was an idea I had in middle school for my theatre class's improv warm-up. The basic idea was you visualized an animal (which didn't have a name at the time) and do something with it, then pass it on to the next person. I decided to make a design for the creature, just for fun. It really stuck with me, and now I made this program based off it.
